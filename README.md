## 🌴 Origin Story (A Tragedy in 3 Acts)

**Act 1 — The Innocent Beginning**
> It was May. It was 42°C. I opened my laptop and said *"I should drink more water this summer."*
> This was my villain origin story.

**Act 2 — The Point of No Return**
> Six hours later I had a vintage Tamil FM radio loading screen, a canvas-drawn spin wheel, Web Audio API synthesised sound effects, XP levels, achievement badges, a cinema ticket recipe sheet, kolam dividers, cracked paint CSS textures, a barber-pole progress bar, postage stamp card borders, a rubber TRIED stamp overlay, and 110 drinks with full step-by-step recipes and ingredient spotlight cards.

**Act 3 — The Reckoning**
> I still hadn't drunk any water.
> The pomegranate juice was on slide 6.
> My amma walked by, saw the screen, said *"innum juice kudikkalaya? computer-la juice kudikka mudiyuma?"* and walked away without waiting for an answer.
> She was right. She is always right. This is documented.

> Like Vikram in *Pithamagan* — I emerged from isolation having done something nobody asked for, and it somehow worked.

## 🏆 What Even Is This

This is a **single HTML file** that does more than most apps on the Play Store, costs ₹0 to run, has zero tracking, zero ads, zero login, and exists entirely because one guy in Dindigul decided a plain notes app was not dramatic enough for his summer hydration journey.

Features that were absolutely necessary:
- ✅ Vintage radio loading screen with real equalizer animation
- ✅ Spinning wheel of fate for drink selection
- ✅ Cinema ticket recipe sheets with perforated tear lines
- ✅ XP points and 7 level tiers from *Sip Seeker* to *GOAT of Sips*
- ✅ Confetti that bursts when you check off a drink
- ✅ Sound effects. For juice. Web Audio API synthesised. From scratch.
- ✅ Rubber stamp TRIED overlay on completed cards
- ✅ Kolam ✦❋✦ patterns between every section
- ✅ Aged paper grain texture because plain white felt too 2015

Features that were NOT included:
- ❌ Actual reminder to drink water
- ❌ Any form of sleep schedule enforcement
- ❌ A second opinion before shipping
- ❌ A therapist

*Because who just loads a page like a normal person? En vazhi, thani vazhi.*

You are greeted by a vintage Tamil FM radio. Spinning dial. Pulsing speaker. Live equalizer. FM frequency changing randomly between 88.9 and 104.7. A scrolling ticker that says *"♪ கோடை காலம் · Summer Special · 110 Drinks ♪"*.

- *"Squeezing the mangoes…"*

### 🎨 The Design Brief (That I Gave Myself at 2 AM)

> *"Retro Tamil. Vintage Madurai cinema poster. Cracked paint. Aged paper. Gold and vermilion. Postage stamps. Kolam patterns. Hand-painted feel. Old Dindigul shop sign. BUT ALSO dark. BUT ALSO cream. BUT ALSO both simultaneously."*

**What actually shipped:**
- **Four fonts** — Abril Fatface · Boogaloo · Tiro Tamil · DM Mono
  *(Three fonts is a choice. Four fonts is a statement. I made a statement.)*
- **Saffron `#F4A300` + Vermilion `#D94F2B`** — exact colour of a Dindigul shop sign at 6 PM golden hour
- **CSS grain overlay on literally everything** — smooth was not the vibe
- **Postage stamp perforated borders** — `outline: 2px dashed` has never felt more powerful
- **Kolam ✦❋✦ dividers** — hand-drawn energy, zero hands involved
- **Cracked paint SVG texture** — the header has seen battles
- **Barber-pole animated progress bar** — a static bar in 2025 is a missed opportunity and I will not apologise

**Amma's full design review:**
*"Enna oru colour? Kaண்ணu koosum. Yaar padikka solra ithaya?"*
*(Translation: "What is this colour? Eyes are burning. Who is going to read this?")*

The colours are staying. The design is final. The README is shipping.
