## 🏆 What Even Is This

This is a **single HTML file** that does more than most apps on the Play Store, costs ₹0 to run, has zero tracking, zero ads, zero login, and exists entirely because one guy in Dindigul decided a plain notes app was not dramatic enough for his summer hydration journey.

Features that were absolutely necessary:
- ✅ Vintage radio loading screen with real equalizer animation
- ✅ Spinning wheel of fate for drink selection
- ✅ Cinema ticket recipe sheets with perforated tear lines
- ✅ XP points and 7 level tiers from *Sip Seeker* to *GOAT of Sips*
- ✅ Confetti that bursts when you check off a drink
- ✅ Sound effects. For juice. Web Audio API synthesised. From scratch.
- ✅ Rubber stamp TRIED overlay on completed cards
- ✅ Kolam ✦❋✦ patterns between every section
- ✅ Aged paper grain texture because plain white felt too 2015

Features that were NOT included:
- ❌ Actual reminder to drink water
- ❌ Any form of sleep schedule enforcement
- ❌ A second opinion before shipping
- ❌ A therapist
