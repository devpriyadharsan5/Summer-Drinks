## 🌴 What even is this?

So I sat in front of my laptop all summer in Dindigul, sweating through my shirt, telling myself I'd "stay hydrated" while sipping the same boring water for the 47th time.

Then I decided to do something about it.

This is a **110-drink interactive tracker** covering every smoothie, juice, sherbet, herbal concoction, and legendary Tamil summer drink you could possibly make or find — from 6 AM amla shots to 10 PM ashwagandha milk.

Built it so I'd actually remember to try them. Turns out making a whole web app is easier than just drinking the juice. Classic.

---

## ✨ Features (fancy word for "stuff it does")

- ✅ **Check off drinks** as you try them — finally, accountability
- ❤️ **Favourite** the ones that actually slap
- 📋 **Full recipe + health benefits** — tap any drink, boom, step-by-step
- 📊 **Dashboard with live stats** — because data analysts don't just drink juice, they *track* juice
- 🔍 **Search** — for when you forget if pomegranate is morning or noon (it's morning, trust)
- 🌅☀️🌇🌙 **Filter by time of day** — Morning / Noon / Evening / Night
- 💾 **Saves your progress** automatically — no login, no nonsense
- 📱 **Add to Home Screen** — lives on your phone like a proper app

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML** — one single file. No React. No framework. Pure old school.

- **Vanilla JS** — because sometimes you don't need a library to check a checkbox
- **localStorage** — your progress stays even if you close the tab. Magic.
- **Google Fonts** — Fraunces + DM Mono because we have *standards*
